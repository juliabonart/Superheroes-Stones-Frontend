Women Superheros-stones

Emerald Woman

Abilities:
Can foresee the future; has wisdom; has a photographic supermemory; emits six green beams as a weapon; can reverse the aging process; removes any negative effects on the eyes (blindness, irritation, photosensitivity, etc.); is the main enemy of all amphibian opponents.
Weaknesses:
Very fragile; vulnerable to blows and fire.

Diamond Woman

Abilities:
Can turn her skin into a diamond layer, which makes it absolutely invulnerable to external influences; capable of refracting sunlight and concentrating it; can create diamond and, using concentrated sunlight, instantly turn it into exploding graphite; has a diamond blade that can cut anything.
Weaknesses:
Excessive self-importance from having no weaknesses.

Sapphire Woman

Abilities:
Her skin is almost impossible to penetrate, almost as invulnerable as Diamond Woman; capable of accumulating dark cosmic energy and using it as a weapon; recognizes and senses at a distance any types of poisons and poisonous creatures, while the color of her dress and hair changes; capable of becoming completely transparent; has extraordinary intuition; has the greatest compassion and empathy than other heroines; has a twin sister, RubyÂ Woman.
Weaknesses:
Constant rivalry with her sister; she can only be defeated by Diamond Woman.

Ruby Woman

Abilities:
She is as difficult to defeat as her sister Sapphire Woman; absorbs sunlight and emits it in the form of laser beams emanating from her hands; in daylight she looks like fire, and at night she illuminates everything around with a bright crimson-red light.
Weaknesses:
Constant rivalry with her sister; can only be defeated by Diamond Woman.

Pearl Woman

Abilities:
Capable of enveloping an enemy in a thin layer of mother-of-pearl, temporarily stopping him completely, or can envelop an ally in battle for protection; when wounded, she also covers the wound with mother-of-pearl; shoots strings of pearls at high speed; causes a feeling of sadness and sorrow in the enemy, often making him cry; can stop bleeding and treat some diseases.
Weaknesses:
Very vulnerable to pressure and force; dissolves instantly when exposed to acid.

Topaz Woman

Abilities:
Very strong in combat, withstands great pressure and force; can force the enemy to tell the truth; is able to calm conflicting parties, often acts as an ambassador or negotiator; a born leader, capable of inspiring others to great deeds; instills in others a sense of happiness and optimism; the radiation enhances her abilities and improves her mood.
Weaknesses:
Becomes sad and loses her abilities from bright sunlight and heat; really doesn't like salt.
