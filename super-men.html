Men Superheros-stones

Gold Man

Abilities:
Capable of storing electricity and solar heat and using it as a weapon; when heated, he can take any shape; enhances the abilities of his younger brother Silver Man.
Weaknesses:
Loses strength and is destroyed by acids; The main enemy is Quicksilver Woman.

Silver Man

Abilities:
Much more resistant to external influences than his brother; able to treat diseases and heal wounds; is not always able to change shape, is able to absorb electricity and heat stronger than his brother, and with his brother’s support he becomes invincible; capable of dispersing clouds to harness solar energy; in love with a Quicksilver Woman, they have a daughter, Amalgam.
Weaknesses:
Quicksilver Woman.

Copper Man

Abilities:
Able to manage electrical devices and energy; attracts lightning and uses their energy; is the main enemy of those who feed on other people's energy; capable of purifying land and water; in open space generates a patina shield that protects it from impacts; often saved the Gold Man and is his friend; is the son of Coal Man and Malachite Woman; capable of resisting Magnetto.
Weaknesses:
Lazy; womanizer, has many illegitimate children; likes to spend time with women more than in battle, becomes defenseless without oxygen or in a confined space.

Bronze Man

Abilities:
Son of the Copper Man and the Tin Woman; unlike his father, he does not need a shield, he is very strong and is able to withstand a lot of pressure and force (he despises his father for his dependence on the shield); in battle, his shield produces a long and clear mesmerizing sound upon impact, causing enemies to slow down; inherited from his father the ability to absorb heat and destroy enemies who feed on other people’s energy, although he does not do this so effectively; for battles in the water he takes Zinc Man as a partner; for battles against fire or explosions, he takes Beryllium Man as a partner; does not age.
Weaknesses:
Unable to fight alone, needs support.

Steel Man

Abilities:
His steel skin is almost impossible to pierce or destroy; capable of absorbing and releasing a stream of destructive radiation; capable of neutralizing various odors and some types of chemical weapons; has an unstable character, on the battlefield there are three stages of mood: at the first stage - he is very calm and confident in victory, then the second stage comes - he becomes irritable and nervous, and in the end - he becomes furious and destroys everything in his path; his faithful friend is the Manganese Man.
Weaknesses:
Low temperatures and liquid nitrogen; excessive exposure to oxygen has a destructive effect on him, but communication with a friend cures him.

Manganese Man

Abilities:
Able to influence the enemy’s nervous system, causing irritability and hallucinations; is able to change color to green, blue, purple and crimson, which helps him blend in with his environment; resistant to external influences and electric shock; able to stabilize the mood and condition of other superheroes; makes his friend the Man of Steel invincible; lives at the bottom of the ocean.
Weaknesses:
Susceptible to destruction by acid; may disintegrate from heating in a vacuum.
